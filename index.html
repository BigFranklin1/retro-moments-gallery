Retro-Moments+

In China, there are 1000 deceased accounts on social net works.

Retro-Moments+

It is foreseeable that the deceased accounts will eventually outnumber the living shortly. Though some sites manage to deal with the dead accounts (Twitter, Facebook, Google .etc), most companies follow the noninterference philosophy since improper policy may be risky.

Retro-Moments+

In this project, we build a virtual space that exhibits some moments of deceased accounts on Weibo, the largest social network site in China, with no policy on dead accounts. We fetched data from some authorized dead accounts by other users. We aim to create an immersive environment for the visitors, and they could understand the passions, loneliness, and dreams of the passed-away owners. Perhaps, our anger and hatred get insignificant.
